ISPM-15 is an international standard for wood packaging material. It requires heat treatment to eliminate pests and is mandatory for international shipping to prevent the spread of wood-boring insects.

The heat treatment process typically takes 24-48 hours, depending on the volume and type of wood. This includes heating to the required temperature and maintaining it for the specified duration.
